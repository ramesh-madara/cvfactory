# Dark Mode Toggle Button

The theme switch sits in the **top-right corner** of the header — a small, calm control that lets visitors flip between a bright daytime look and a deep evening look.

---

## Shape

The button is a **perfect circle** — smooth and symmetrical, like a coin or a lens cap.

On phones it feels compact but still easy to tap. On larger screens it grows just slightly, so it stays balanced beside the navigation without dominating the header.

```
        ┌─────────┐
        │    ☾    │   ← round, icon centered
        └─────────┘
           circle
```

There is no text inside — only a single icon at the heart of the circle.

---

## Surface & material

In **light mode**, the circle reads as **frosted glass**: softly white, slightly see-through, with a gentle blur behind it. A thin, pale edge traces the rim, and a light shadow lifts it just off the header — as if it is floating on the bar.

In **dark mode**, the same shape becomes **smoky glass**: a dark, translucent disk with a faint silvery border. It still feels glassy, but quieter — it belongs on a night-time interface.

The whole header shares this glass language, so the toggle feels like part of the furniture, not a sticker pasted on.

---

## The icon

The icon tells you what will happen when you tap — not where you already are.

| You are viewing | You see | Tap to move toward |
|-----------------|---------|---------------------|
| Light, bright page | **Moon** ☾ | Darker, evening mood |
| Dark, dim page | **Sun** ☀ | Brighter, daytime mood |

The moon is a soft crescent silhouette; the sun is a simple radiant disc. Both are small, centered, and uncluttered — easy to read at a glance.

When the theme changes, the icon **swaps instantly** to the opposite symbol, like turning a sign to point the other way.

---

## Hover & press

**Hover** (mouse or trackpad): the glass brightens a little — the circle becomes slightly more opaque and welcoming, as if catching more light.

**Press / tap**: the circle **shrinks inward** for a brief moment — a subtle squeeze, like pressing a physical button. It springs back when you release. This gives a tactile, satisfying click without being flashy.

Colors on the icon stay soft grey in light mode and pale silver in dark mode, so the control stays elegant rather than loud.

---

## What happens to the page

One tap **floods the entire site** into the other mood:

**Light → Dark**  
The background sinks into deep charcoal. Text turns soft white. Glass panels become darker smoke. The moon becomes a sun.

**Dark → Light**  
The background opens into soft off-white. Text turns rich black. Glass panels turn milky and bright. The sun becomes a moon.

The change is **smooth and whole-page** — not just the button, but galleries, cards, footer, and modal all shift together in one calm breath.

Your choice is **remembered** when you return, so the site greets you in the same mood you left it.

---

## Placement in the header

The header is a horizontal glass bar fixed at the top. Three zones balance across it:

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  CV FACTORY.LK      [ CVs | Feedback ]      (☾) │
│     logo                 center toggle      right │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

The theme toggle anchors the **right edge**, mirroring the logo on the left. The CV / Feedback switch sits in the middle. On mobile, spacing is tightened so the circle still has breathing room from the screen edge — it never feels cramped or clipped.

---

## Overall character

The dark mode toggle is **minimal, round, and glassy**. It does not shout. It whispers: *tap me to change the lighting.*

It matches the rest of CV Factory.LK — premium, restrained, and polished — like a dimmer switch in a well-designed room.
